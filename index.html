Help

WASD or arrow keys = move
Up = enter doors
Space or Enter = Action (read signs, punch)
Click = place items
Bits found naturally underground:

GREEN bits are special, you can jump up through them.

Bits that must be created:


Tiles: choose from tiles in a set on the right to place background blocks.
Think of it like wallpaper, since it layers on top of bits.

Block Eraser: removes blocks, uncovering any bits behind.


Switch Block: swaps white and black bits
Door 1: creates your own room
Door 2: creates your own game level
Door 3: creates your own vertical game level
Goomba: enemy you can place in your own areas
Coin: to collect in your levels.
YouTube TV: plays a youtube video or playlist. It stays in sync with the owner.
MakerMeow: insert bits to create new items.
